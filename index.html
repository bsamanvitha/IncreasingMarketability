About

Our client was CyberGirlz, an organization created by the STEM Center (SJSU) to inspire middle-school girls to engage in the exciting fields of Computer Science and Cybersecurity. Cybergirlz was a relatively new organization, and has gained attention from the media as the organization was doing well, but compared to other clubs with similar goals and responsibilities, CyberGirlz was so popular and well-known.

Goals

Our team was responsible for helping CyberGirlz reach their goals. Goals were to:

Suggest tools and platforms

One of the main tasks included to research the best tools and platforms suitable to our client's needs and requirements, and to suggest how to use those tools for success.

Help reach out a bigger audience

In order to reach out to a bigger audience, we presented statistics of how social media can help in branding and expansion, and helped our client make a decision as to why updating social media is important.

Teach how to use the tools

As technology ambassadors, we were responsible for teaching them how and why to leverage technology for solving business problems. We taught our client how to use use use tools to automate tasks.
